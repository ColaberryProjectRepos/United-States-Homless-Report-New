Positive and Negative Insights 5

Positive Insights :






  • The average homeless for the region of Northeast from the year of 2018 to 2019 decreased by 8.81%, Income increased on average by 5.01% in the same period, inflation in the country  was 1.81% in 2019.



  • The average homeless for the region of Midwest from the year of 2018 to 2019 decreased by 0.91%, Income increased on average by 3.84% in the same period, meanwhile inflation in the country  was 1.81% in 2019.



Negative Insighs :







  • The average homeless for the region of West from the year of 2018 to 2019 Increased by 1.72%





  •  Over 50% of the average homeless in the United States, in 2019, were ages over 24.



The above points show some of the positive and the negative insights from the analysis of the data.
