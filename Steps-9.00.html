Creating Measures


In this step you will create Measures to the imported tables
 
 
 
 
 
1.    Right click your "US Data by State" and click on "New Measure" at the top. In this table, you will create 10 different measures using the following DAX codes to get Averages of  Total Homeless, Income, Population and Averages for their YOY% . You can also add the refresh date measure here to get the current date. See the GIF how to do measures
 
 
 
Average Homeless = Sum('US Data By State'[Overall Homeless])/DISTINCTCOUNT('US Data By State'[YEAR]) 
 
Average Ages 18 to 24 = SUM('US Data By State'[Overall Homeless - Age 18 to 24])/DISTINCTCOUNT('US Data By State'[YEAR])
 
Average Ages less than 18 = SUM('US Data By State'[Overall Homeless - Under 18])/DISTINCTCOUNT('US Data By State'[YEAR])
 
Average Ages Over 24 = SUM('US Data By State'[Overall Homeless - Over 24])/DISTINCTCOUNT('US Data By State'[YEAR])
 
 
Average Income = CALCULATE(AVERAGE('US Data By State'[Median Income]),'US Data By State'[YEAR ID] <> 0)
 
 
Average Population = sum('US Data By State'[Population])/DISTINCTCOUNT('US Data By State'[YEAR])
 
Homeless YOY% = CALCULATE(AVERAGE('US Data By State'[YOY% Total Homeless]),'US Data By State'[YEAR ID] <> 1)
 
Income YOY% = CALCULATE(AVERAGE('US Data By State'[Income YOY%]),'US Data By State'[YEAR ID] <> 1)
 
Population YOY% = CALCULATE(AVERAGE('US Data By State'[Population YOY%]),'US Data By 
State'[YEAR ID] <> 1)
 
 
Refresh Date = TODAY()
 
 
 
2.   Click your "Total US Data" and click on "New Measure" at the top. In this table, you will create  4 different measures using the following DAX codes to get averages for their YOY% for Homeless, Income, Population, and  Inflation

 
HomelessYOY% = SUM('Total US Data'[Homeless YOY%])/DISTINCTCOUNT('Total US Data'[Year])
 
IncomeYOY% = SUM('Total US Data'[M.Income YOY%])/DISTINCTCOUNT('Total US Data'[Year])
 
Population YOY% = SUM('Total US Data'[Population YOY%])/DISTINCTCOUNT('Total US Data'[Year])
 
Inflationrate = SUM('Total US Data'[  US Inflation Rate ])/DISTINCTCOUNT('Total US Data'[Year])
